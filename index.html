About Us

WealthHealth AI Inc is an AI product company based in Irvine, California. We believe powerful technology should be accessible to everyone — not just those with a technical background.

Every product we build is designed to be private by default, easy to use from day one, and powerful enough to grow with you. No accounts with third-party AI companies. No data leaving your device unless you choose.

Founded by Jason Li, PhD (Biomedical Sciences, UCI) with a mission to make AI genuinely useful for how people actually live and work.
